Vitreo Retinal Surgery

Vitreo retnial surgery does not refer to one specific type of surgery. It refers to any surgical procedure that treats eye problems involving the retna, macula and vitreous fluid. These vision disorders include mascular degeneration, retinal detachment, and diabetic retinopathy.
