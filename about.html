About OneDoc

Get the Healthcare You Need.

OneDoc is an online marketplace to help consumers find the health care they need. OneDoc is designed for consumers who shop for their own health care, either paying their own way or having a high deductible insurance plan. We believe more and more Americans over time will have control over their own health care purchasing decisions.

Our mission is to inform the individual’s choice of a physician, surge, health facility, hospital, or health system, and subsequently to empower that relationship.
We believe empowering that relationship means providing price transparency. By showing price information, we then allow the smart health shopper to focus on what matters: finding the health care that works for him or her. When price is “murky” and unknown that’s impossible to do. You shouldn’t need a PhD to figure out how much health care should cost!

On our site, don’t expect all the prices for a given service to be the same. We want prices to vary. We want providers of health care to talk about what distinguishes their care, what motivates them to wake up every morning and help people. We know that price isn’t the only decision maker for you, but it’s important, and by knowing price, you can then consider all the other factors that are important for your individual decision.

Technology cannot replace the human interaction of health care. Our goal is to use our technology to help put you in front of a human being, and then we let the technology get out of the way!
