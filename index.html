How it Works

Healthcare Made Simple.

The OneDoc marketplace allows you to search for everything from a basic checkup in a doctor’s office to a complex procedure in a hospital or surgery center. Apples-to-apples comparisons can be made and pricing information is provided so you can find the health care that works for you.

RealDocTalk

Tired of all the Double Talk? Introducing RealDocTalk.

No sugarcoating. What is the outcome that matters, and how can you and the doctor work together to achieve it?
